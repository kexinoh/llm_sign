## Reporting a Vulnerability

At this time, vulnerability reports are not eligible for any bounty rewards. However, we sincerely appreciate every report submitted by security researchers.

You are welcome to report vulnerabilities directly through GitHub Security Reports. Our team will acknowledge and review reports within 5 business days. Please provide a detailed proof of concept (PoC) and clearly describe the potential impact whenever possible.

The following scenarios are considered out of scope in our threat model:

1. Any tampering of messages performed internally by the vendor itself.
2. Any compromise of message confidentiality, as message signatures are intended to guarantee integrity only.
